A little bit about me

From my humble beginings in video production, to chasing other dreams and landing on product design.

The beginings - circa 2001/2002

From a young age I was a, well what my mum used to say a hyperactive and curious kid. My dad was an avid skier and taught me to ski virtually before I could run at the age of only 2. By the time I was 6, Pepsi, yes THE Pepsi drinks was my skiing sponsor and I was a small kid wonder when it came to this beautiful sport.

Fun little story, when we were kids around this time, my sister always wanted to go as fast as I went, so one time she did. And she ended up in the restaurant at the bottom of the ski track, because she couldn't break fast enough. We laugh at this story to this day.

Life happened, and I grew further apart from skiing at a professional capacity and sort of went on with my life, whilst taking every opportunity to go skiing for my pure joy.

My background in video production

As high school was coming to an end, I was left thinking if I'd wanted to go on with studying or if I should set about doing something a bit different then the rest of my friends.

So, my dad, being as supportive as he ever was towards me went on and bough me my first DJI drone so I could start "something" with that.

This was 2015, and drones were still a novelty. So I was lucky to have been one of the first in Croatia to start up my drone business. Filming the vast beauty of Croatia and trying to sell stock videos and images I was mostly earning my buck by filming small business's, wineries, and the many apartments around the costal Croatia.

Somewhere along the way, I figured maybe it was time to get myself a proper drone, so I got one of the heavy duty DJI drones, and some camera's to go along and went on to work on some of the most amazing projects that came along not only in Croatia, but around much of the Europe.

In my free time, you'll find me...

Usually I spend my free time with my partner and our golden boy, Pickles at parks, or the beach.

I have been training martial arts most of my live, mma through high school and more recently I find great joy in muay thai. There is just something about this sport, the excitement, having to think in this very moment what you will do next, or risk well, getting hit by your opponent.
